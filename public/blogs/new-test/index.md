## Three-Level Hierarchy Explained

```
Area → Category → Subcategory → [Your Content]
```

| Level | What It Is | How Many | Example |
|-------|-----------|----------|---------|
| **Area** | Broadest domain of knowledge | 6 active + 3 reserved | Technology |
| **Category** | Major topic within an area | ~4-5 per area | Artificial Intelligence |
| **Subcategory** | Specific focus within a category | ~5-6 per category | LLMs & Language Models |
